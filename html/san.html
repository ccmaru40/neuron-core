The NINJA

        Ninja dogs are often imagined as highly trained, stealthy canines that
        combine the agility and loyalty of dogs with the disciplined skillset of
        ninjas. In many cartoons, comics, and folklore-inspired stories, these
        dogs are portrayed as swift, intelligent warriors who wear miniature
        ninja outfits, perform acrobatics, and carry out secret missions under
        the cover of night. Their heightened senses, such as smell and hearing,
        make them perfect for tracking enemies or detecting danger before it
        strikes. Some tales even show them using special tools like smoke bombs
        or shurikens, adding to their mystique and charm.
